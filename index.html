How the partnership works

Many businesses have parking lots that are empty after closing. We lease and operate chargers during those unused hours. For example, if a bank is open from 9 AM to 5 PM, the chargers would be active only from 5 PM to 9 AM. During the bank's business hours, the chargers remain turned off. This ensures no impact to customer parking or daily operations. You earn additional income from space you already have, with no operational responsibility.
